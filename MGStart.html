Matching Mania

Objective:
Try to match the cards in the least amount of moves.

Directions:
- Click two cards and if they are a match, the pair will stay facing up.
- If the two cards do not match, they will flip back over to the cover.
- One click is one move, so think wisely!

Easy Level: 24 moves
Medium Level: 30 moves
Hard Level: 34 moves
